TechStore Employee Handbook — HR Policies
Version 3.2 | Effective March 2025

Chapter 1: Paid Time Off (PTO)

Full-time employees accrue PTO at the following rates based on years of service:
- 0-2 years: 15 days per year (1.25 days per month)
- 3-5 years: 20 days per year (1.67 days per month)
- 6+ years: 25 days per year (2.08 days per month)

PTO requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal. Requests during peak retail periods (Black Friday week, last two weeks of December) require manager approval at least 30 days in advance. Unused PTO may be carried over to the following year, up to a maximum of 5 days. PTO balances exceeding the carry-over limit are forfeited on January 1. Upon separation from the company, accrued but unused PTO is paid out at the employee's current rate.

Chapter 2: Remote Work Policy

Corporate and technology employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Retail store employees are not eligible for remote work. Remote employees must be available during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM local time) and responsive on Slack within 30 minutes. Remote work arrangements can be revoked with 2 weeks notice if performance concerns arise. Employees working remotely are responsible for maintaining a secure, private workspace and a reliable internet connection. The company provides a one-time $500 home office stipend for newly approved remote workers.

Chapter 3: Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted twice per year: mid-year (June) and year-end (December). Reviews use the 5-point scale: Exceeds Expectations (5), Meets Expectations High (4), Meets Expectations (3), Needs Improvement (2), Unsatisfactory (1). Employees receiving a rating of 2 or below are placed on a 90-day Performance Improvement Plan (PIP). Two consecutive ratings of 2 or below may result in termination. Year-end reviews determine annual merit increases and bonus eligibility. Merit increases range from 0-8% based on performance rating and company financial performance.

Chapter 4: Benefits

Health insurance coverage begins on the first day of the month following 30 days of employment. TechStore offers three health plan options: Basic (employee pays 10% of premium), Standard (employee pays 20%), and Premium (employee pays 30%). Dental and vision insurance are included in all plans. The company matches 401(k) contributions up to 4% of salary. Employees receive a 25% discount on all TechStore products, up to $2,000 per year in total discount value. The company provides $50,000 in basic life insurance at no cost to the employee.

Chapter 5: Code of Conduct

Employees must treat customers and colleagues with respect and professionalism at all times. Discrimination, harassment, or bullying based on race, gender, age, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or any protected characteristic will not be tolerated and is grounds for immediate termination. Confidential company information, including financial data, customer information, and strategic plans, must not be shared outside the company. Use of company systems for personal business is limited to reasonable, occasional use. Employees must report any suspected fraud, theft, or policy violations to their manager or the anonymous ethics hotline.

Chapter 6: Leave of Absence

Medical leave: Employees with 12+ months of tenure are eligible for up to 12 weeks of unpaid medical leave under FMLA. Short-term disability provides 60% of base salary for up to 26 weeks. Parental leave: Primary caregivers receive 12 weeks of paid leave. Secondary caregivers receive 4 weeks of paid leave. Bereavement leave: 5 days for immediate family members, 3 days for extended family. Jury duty: Full pay for up to 10 days of jury service per year.
